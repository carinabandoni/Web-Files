I interned at TrueCoach and worked closely with the senior designer to make Google advertisements, social media, and blog graphics.

Train Your Clients Online With The #1 Platform Built For Personal Trainers

TrueCoach is simply a personal training app. Their mission is to help coaches save time, deliver a better experience for their clients, and grow their training businesses. TrueCoach was built to enable 1-to-1 coaching that scales, freeing up time for coaches to get out of the gym and work on their businesses rather than in their businesses.
