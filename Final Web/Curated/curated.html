Curated

I worked with Curated on the marketing team as a freelance graphic designer. I helped them create advertisements, emails, and social media graphics.

Curated is an online shopping service that helps you find the right product - something that uniquely and perfectly fits you, and helps you enjoy the world to its fullest. The sports that they focus on helping you get the best product for are skis, snowboards, golf, cycling, camping and hiking, fly fishing conventional fishing, and tennis. Curated uses experts to help you find these products.

They work hard to maintain a strong expert community and think of a great expert as someone who has the expertise, empathy, and enthusiasm. Getting to find the perfect product requires the thoughtful guidance of a real person with the right expertise who takes the time to learn about your needs and only recommends products they believe in.
