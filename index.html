Here are 5 Places to Take a Photoshoot in Denver

  • Union Station
    • Union Station has to be one of the most photographable train stations there is. The interior and exterior of the building are both stunning. You can snap a picture in front of the iconic union station sign, or inside at the dainty flower shop. The options here are endless.
  • Pastel Row on Colfax Ave
    • Pastel Row is a collection of townhomes all painted in vibrant, pastel colors. If you are looking to add some color to your Instagram feed, this is the place for you.
  • Red Rocks Amphitheater
    • If you’ve ever stepped foot into Colorado, we know you’ve heard of Red Rocks. While it’s 10 miles west of Denver, we HAD to include this iconic spot. Whether its a sunrise photo or selfies from a concert, the photos here are breathtaking. You won’t regret checking out this spot!
  • Denver Botanic Gardens
    • At the Denver Botanic Gardens, you can explore 24 acres of picture-perfect spots. There is a different photogenic bridge, plant, and gazebo around every corner. You HAVE to stop here if you want a new nature spot to switch up your scenery.
